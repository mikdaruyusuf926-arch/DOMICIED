About Domicied School

Domicied School is committed to building a safe, inspiring learning environment for students from elementary through high school. Our dedicated faculty blends academic excellence with character education, preparing learners for the future with confidence and curiosity.

We foster a culture of teamwork, creativity, and community involvement. Students benefit from rigorous coursework, supportive mentoring, and pubic speech experience that encourages leadership and lifelong learning.

History

Dr. Mohamed Ibn Chambas Institute Of Education was established in the year 2017 with an enrollment of One Hundred And Six pupils(106) and Nine (9) teacher. it has its vision as "To acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them as lifelong learners to participate in, and contribte to the global world, to practice the core values of school that respect, tolerate, and inclusive excellence in all spheres of life, to become the leading educational institution in Northern Ghana with acadamic excellence", it also has its motto as First Among Equals, as a private school it has been registered with the National School Inspectorate Authority (NASIA), Ghana Education Service (GES), Nanumba Municipal Assembly And the Registrar General Dept.
