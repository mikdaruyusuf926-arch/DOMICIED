About Domicied School

Domicied School is committed to building a safe, inspiring learning environment for students from elementary through high school. Our dedicated faculty blends academic excellence with character education, preparing learners for the future with confidence and curiosity.

We foster a culture of teamwork, creativity, and community involvement. Students benefit from rigorous coursework, supportive mentoring, and pubic speech experience that encourages leadership and lifelong learning.

02

ABOUT SCHOOL

VISION

To acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them as lifelong learners to participate in, and contribte to the global world, to practice the core values of school that respect, tolerate, and inclusive excellence in all spheres of life, to become the leading educational institution in Northern Ghana with acadamic excellence.

MISSION

Develop the people with active and creative minds to enhance equal opporturnities for all children of school going age, to access quality education from preparatory to second cycle schools through service deliveries (Teaching and Learning) to act independently.

HISTORY

Physical education, team sports, and wellness initiatives build healthy habits and strong teamwork.

Community Learning

Service projects, clubs, and leadership programs encourage students to engage with their neighborhood and world.
