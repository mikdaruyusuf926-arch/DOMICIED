Arrival: Phones must be turned off or silenced before entering the school gates.
Storage: Devices must remain inside student lockers or designated homeroom cubbies all day.
Classrooms: Cell phones are strictly banned from classrooms, libraries, and science labs.
Breaks: Students may not use phones during morning recess or lunch periods.
Exceptions: Teachers may grant temporary access solely for approved educational projects.
Emergencies: Parents must contact the front office to relay urgent messages to students.
Consequences
First Offense: The device is confiscated and returned to the student at the end of the day.
Second Offense: The device is held in the main office until a parent or guardian collects it.
Third Offense: The student loses the privilege of bringing a phone to school for the term.
